Our Love Story

Aidan and Theo met at Snow College, in Ethics Bowl. If you don't know what that is, we highly recommend you look into it. Theo thought Aidan was cute, cool, and wanted to get to know him. Aidan found Theo fascinating, the look, the personality, and was intruiged from the beginning.

Neither of us new how to approach, and for a little while, we were just passerbys. It's funny how the things we are interested in bring us together. Hobbies, interests, school, ideas. They guide our paths and make unlikely friendships.

During one of these meetings in ethics bowl, Aidan had to leave early to run a game of Dungeons and Dragons. A few of the members of Ethics Bowl, including Theo, was looking for just such a game to join for a while. Common interests brought these players together. As it was announced that Aidan was leaving, Theo and others expressed interest in the game, and got an invite.

It was a fantastic game indeed, reaching up to 7 consistent hobbyists gathering weekly over 8 months. They formed friendships and began to get together for more than just game nights. Through this, Aidan and Theo formed a friendship, and caught feelings after that.

For a while, neither of us knew that the other was interested. Both complained to mutual friends, who after a while finally decided to push us together.

Talking together, we realized that we had so much more in common than we had thought. Views of the world which just made sense. For Aidan, Theo was in his mind, a real vision of what the world ought to be, what was right with it. Theo found a deep well of love for Aidan, one that just never seemed to run out.

We started dating in January 2025, and made time for eachother, whenever possible. Through, spring, summer, and into fall of 2025, our relationship only became stronger, and our love deepened.

On September 26, 2025, Aidan proposed to Theo, who enthusiastically agreed.

We made lifetime plans together. Looked at the road ahead and planned for success. Great education, Great jobs, a floorplan for a Great Home. Only three and a half months later, life did as life does.

Consistently, Theo began to have left-side hip pain. One day, when Aidan came home, Theo was hurting so bad that we had to go to the ER. Soon after, we were referred to a cancer specialist, as they suspected a bad cancer.

On January 13th, Theo was diagnosed with Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma. We didn't know what this meant for us, and it left us very uncertain, but soon after our bad news, we decided that if this was the hand we were dealt, we were going to play the cards. It did us no use to be negative, that would just make it worse for us, so we focused on the positive, and on our love for each other. With support from the community, and from close family, we waded bravely into the uncertainty without looking back. We agreed to be there, and give 100%. Aidan had to temporarily leave his bachelor's program at Snow College. Theo got put on what amounted to indefinite leave from work. Still, we looked forward.

We quickly got into medical treatment, and modern medicine spread its wings, as we got the best news possible. The treatments were working well. We have many people to thank for our success story, which will only grow as time goes on.

And now we're getting married in September 2026, on the 26th, just as planned.
